## AWS Customer Carbon Footprint Tool

The **AWS Customer Carbon Footprint Tool** helps organizations **track, measure, review, and forecast** the carbon emissions associated with their **AWS cloud usage**.  
It enables customers to monitor their **environmental impact** and progress toward **sustainability goals**, such as achieving **100% renewable energy usage**.

---

### Purpose

| Objective                        | Description                                                                                      |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Track Carbon Emissions**       | Understand the total amount of CO₂ generated from your AWS workloads.                            |
| **Measure & Review**             | View emissions data across services, regions, and time periods.                                  |
| **Forecast Impact**              | Estimate future carbon emissions based on current and projected AWS usage.                       |
| **Support Sustainability Goals** | Demonstrate and report your organization's journey toward net-zero and renewable energy targets. |

---

### Accessing the Tool

1. Sign in to the **AWS Management Console**.
2. Navigate to **Billing and Cost Management**.
3. In the search bar, type **“carbon”**.
4. Select **Customer Carbon Footprint Tool** under **Features**.

---

### Key Features

| Feature                              | Description                                                                            |
| ------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Emission Summary**                 | Displays total carbon emissions for a selected period (start month → end month).       |
| **Savings Overview**                 | Shows emission reductions achieved through AWS’s ongoing renewable energy initiatives. |
| **Breakdown by Geography & Service** | Identifies which AWS Regions and Services contribute most to emissions.                |
| **Time-Based Trends**                | Graphs showing emissions and progress toward **100% renewable energy** over time.      |
| **Forecast View**                    | Projects future emissions based on usage patterns.                                     |

---

### Benefits

| Benefit                           | Description                                                                      |
| --------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Transparency**                  | Provides clear visibility into the environmental impact of your cloud workloads. |
| **Compliance & Reporting**        | Helps meet regulatory or corporate sustainability reporting requirements.        |
| **Optimization Insights**         | Identifies high-emission workloads that can be optimized or migrated.            |
| **Supports Net-Zero Initiatives** | Tracks AWS’s renewable energy progress and how it affects your footprint.        |

---

### Example Console View

When you open the tool, you can view:

- **Start Month** and **End Month** selectors
- **Emission Summary** (Total CO₂ equivalent)
- **Savings by Region and Service**
- **Historical Graphs** showing emissions over time
- **Path to 100% Renewable Energy** progress indicator

_(If your account doesn’t have data yet, results may show “No data available.”)_
