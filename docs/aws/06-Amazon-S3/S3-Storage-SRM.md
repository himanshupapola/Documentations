AWS is responsible for securing and maintaining the **global S3 storage infrastructure**, ensuring durability, availability, and compliance across all regions.

- Managing **physical infrastructure** (data centers, hardware, networking)
- Ensuring **11 nines (99.999999999%) durability**
- Maintaining **high availability** and **fault tolerance** (can sustain loss of two facilities)
- Performing **infrastructure security**, patching, and access control for AWS personnel
- Ensuring **compliance** with global standards (ISO, SOC, PCI, etc.)
